About Mining Stock Report

{{ SITE_TAGLINE }}

Mining Stock Report exists for one type of investor: the self-directed retail investor who is serious about junior miners, reads the technical report rather than just the press release, and has been burned at least once by a newsletter pump.

Every company on this site goes through the Verdict Framework — a five-factor scorecard covering management alignment, geology quality, capital structure health, catalyst proximity, and comparable acquisition value. The output is one of three verdicts: BUY, WATCH, or AVOID.

There are no IR relationships here. No paid placements. No undisclosed compensation. When that changes — if it does — it will be disclosed prominently on every piece of content it touches.

The 5-Factor Verdict Framework
  1. Management skin-in-the-game — Do insiders own meaningful shares purchased at market?
  2. Project geology quality — What resource classification? What grade and scale?
  3. Capital structure health — Share count, warrant overhang, cash runway.
  4. Catalyst proximity — How close is the next material news event?
  5. Comparable acquisition value — What does the P/NAV look like vs. peer acquisitions?
