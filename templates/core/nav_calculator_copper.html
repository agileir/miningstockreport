How It Works
  1. Pull deposit inputs from the company's NI 43-101 technical report — tonnage by category, grade (% Cu), recovery, opex, capex, mine life.
  2. Pick the study stage — PEA, PFS, or FS.
  3. Enter up to four copper-price benchmarks.
  4. Review the matrix — three NAV scenarios across every price benchmark.
Conversions
  • Grade %: copper percent by weight. 1% Cu ≈ 22.05 lb/tonne.
  • Tonnes: metric tonnes (2,204.62 lb).
  • Price: $/lb LME or COMEX spot. Different futures markets can diverge.
